Tomato Egg Stir-fry

This Chinese tomato egg stir-fry is something you’ll find on many Chinese home dinner tables. It’s fast, easy, cheap, and made with very simple, everyday ingredients.

Ingredients

  • 4 small tomatoes
  • 1 scallion
  • 4 eggs
  • 3/4 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp white pepper
  • 1/2 tsp sesame oil
  • 1 tsp shaoxing wine
  • 3 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 2 tsp sugar
  • 1/2 cup water

Directions

  1. Cutt tomatoes into small wedges and finely chop the scallion.
  2. Crack 4 eggs into a bowl and season with salt, pepper, sesame oil and shaoxing wine. Beat eggs.
  3. Medium heat work, add 2 tablespoons of oil and immediately add the eggs. scramble the eggs and remove.
  4. Add 1 tablespoon oil to work, turn up heat to high, add tomatoes and scallions. Stir-fry for 1 minute, add sugar, salt and water. Add the cooked eggs.
  5. Mix all together, cover the wok and cook for 1-2 minutes.
  6. Uncover, continue to stir over high heat until the sauce thickens to your liking.
